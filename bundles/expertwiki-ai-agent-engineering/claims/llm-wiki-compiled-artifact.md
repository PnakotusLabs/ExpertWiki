# Claim

Karpathy's LLM Wiki pattern treats the wiki as a persistent, compounding
artifact maintained between raw sources and user queries, rather than
re-deriving synthesis from scratch on every query.

# Why It Matters

ExpertWiki should compile and maintain knowledge over time instead of behaving
only like a query-time retrieval layer.
